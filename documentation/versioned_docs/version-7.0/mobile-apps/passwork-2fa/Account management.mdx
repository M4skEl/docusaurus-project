## Add an account

There are two ways you can add an account into **Passwork 2FA**:

- Scan a QR code
- Enter a setup key

If you scan a QR code, the account will be added automatically. If you choose to enter a key, you will need to fill in the account information manually:

## Edit account information

To edit an account, tap the three dots on the right side of the account block and choose **Edit.**

<div style={{ textAlign: 'center' }}>
  <img
    src="/img/assets/QKZtfOnnubSbfN3D1EPqb-pn5q6qT_uoOEt7y3Yj9Xu-20240909-125251.png"
    width="600"
    style={{ display: 'block', margin: '0 auto' }}
  />
</div>

:::tip
1. Tap the three dots and choose **Edit**  
2. Edit the necessary fields in the pop-up window and save the changes
:::

## Delete an account

To delete an account, tap the three dots on the right side of the account block and choose **Delete**.

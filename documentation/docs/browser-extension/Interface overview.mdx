After launching the extension, you will see its **main screen**.

Here you can view your vaults and their contents, create passwords and search through them.

## Action panel

Action panel can be found at the bottom of the extension. It is accessible from any screen and includes [Recent](./passwords/Recents.mdx) and [Starred](./passwords/Starred.mdx) passwords, [Password generator](./passwords/Password generator.mdx), **Notifications** and [Settings](./Settings.mdx):

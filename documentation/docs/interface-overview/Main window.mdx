The main window of Passwork is divided into three sections: the **Content pane** on the right side, the **Navigation pane** on the left and the **Top panel**.

**Top panel** contains the company and user name, Settings and users menu, Account settings, and quick access buttons for notifications and background tasks.

**Navigation pane** allows quick access to the most frequently used sections of Passwork:

- [Search bar:](./navigation-pane/Search.mdx) A search tool to quickly find password by keywords, tags and color labels.
- [Recents:](./navigation-pane/Recents.mdx) A list of recently used passwords in chronological order.
- [Starred:](./navigation-pane/Starred.mdx) A list of favorite passwords marked with a star label for quick access.
- [Inbox:](./navigation-pane/Inbox.mdx) A list of passwords received from other Passwork users.
- [Organization vaults:](./navigation-pane/Organization vaults.mdx) The main type of vault suitable for most tasks.
- [Private vaults:](./navigation-pane/Private vaults.mdx) Personal user vaults that are only accessible to their owners and the users they have added.
- [Bin:](./navigation-pane/Bin.mdx) Container for deleted passwords and folders.

**Content pane** shows the elements you selected in the Navigation pane or Top panel.

:::info
At the bottom of the window, you will find Passwork version info, link to the Help center, and [appearance](./Appearance.mdx) customization menu.
:::

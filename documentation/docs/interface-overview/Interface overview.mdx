The main window of Passwork is divided into [Content pane](https://manuals.passwork.ru/oblast-prosmotra) on the right side of the window, [Navigation pane](https://manuals.passwork.ru/kolonka-navigacii) and [Top panel](https://manuals.passwork.ru/verhnyaya-panel).

**Top panel** contains the company and user name, Settings and users menu, [Account settings](docId:4tsdGid9EUDLljvxTydo2), and quick access buttons for notifications and Background tasks.

**Navigation pane** allows quick access to the most frequently used sections of Passwork:

- [Search bar](docId:rH_nvv3prSnhJ1TPitK-J) — quickly find the desired password by keywords and color labels.
- [Recents](./navigation-pane/Recents.mdx) — a list of recently used passwords in chronological order.
- [Inbox](./navigation-pane/Inbox.mdx) — a list of passwords received from other users.
- [Starred](./navigation-pane/Starred.mdx) — a list of passwords marked with a special color label for quick access.
- [Organization vaults](./navigation-pane/Organization vaults.mdx) — vaults that are accessible to both users and Passwork administrators.
- [Private vaults](docId:eru2pwssLrKqPZAuxLJTb) — vaults that are only accessible to their owner and the users they have added.
- [Bin](./navigation-pane/Bin.mdx) — container for deleted passwords and folders.

Content pane may contain various elements depending on what is selected in the **Navigation** pane or **Top panel**.

:::info
At the bottom of the window, you can find Passwork version info, link to the Help Center, and [Appearance](./Appearance.mdx) customization menu.
:::

## Password actions

### Quick actions

Some password actions, such as adding it to Starred, sharing or editing are available directly in the **password card**. Moving, copying, renaming and deleting passwords is done through a separate **password extra actions** menu.

#### Share password

The mobile app lets you share the password with someone who is not registered in Passwork. To do so, tap the **Generate link** button in password quick actions.

In the pop-up window, set the expiration time and choose the link type: **reusable** and **one-time**. A one-time link expires in three minutes after the user opens it.

#### Bookmark password

Click on the **Star icon** to add the Password to [Starred](./Starred.mdx).

### Extra actions

Here you can move, copy and delete password. To access these options, open the [extra actions](./Password management.mdx#extra-actions) menu.

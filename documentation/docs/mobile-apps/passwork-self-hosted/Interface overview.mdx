## Main screen

After installing and launching the app, you will see its **main screen**.

Here you can view your vaults and their contents, search through passwords and access your notifications and settings.

## Action panel

Action panel can be found at the bottom of the app. It is accessible from any screen and includes [Recent](./passwords/Recents.mdx) and [Starred](./passwords/Starred.mdx) passwords, [Password generator](./passwords/Password generator.mdx) and a button for quick password creation:

## Notifications

Tap on the bell icon in the top-right corner of the main screen to open **Notifications.** Whenever passwords get changed or someone sends you a password, you will see it in the notification list.

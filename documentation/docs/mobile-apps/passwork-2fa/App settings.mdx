In **Passwork 2FA** settings you can:

- **Enable biometrics** to secure your profile with biometric data stored on your device.
- **Change the language** of the app. Passwork 2FA is currently available in English, German and Russian.
- **Change the appearance** for an optimal experience. You can choose between system (changes depending on the phone settings), light and dark themes.
- **Import and export** application data. Using this option you can quickly transfer saved account data from one device to another in the form of a QR code.
